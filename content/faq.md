### Who am I?

I'm a software developer. 

### What is this site?

At the moment, not much but I plan to post content to it more regularly. Mostly my
thoughts and discoveries around programming. Don't expect anything ground breaking to be
discussed, I probably use this for something like micro-blogging and if that is of
interest to others or is helpful, then all the better.

From a technical perspective this is a static website built with
[Zola](https://www.getzola.org) served using Netlify using the
[Zap](https://www.getzola.org/themes/zap/) theme. I plan slowly migrate to something I've
made myself but this will do for now.

### What are your interests?

Broadly speaking, I like to working on and exploring low level programs, video
games, compilers and other performance and security oriented systems and wish to dabble
with hardware in the future.
